Writer Promotion


White Panda believes in rewarding consistency and good performance. Writer promotion is determined by a credit based system.

1. Good Editor reviews earn you credits
2. Good Client reviews earn you credits.
3. Taking Jobs regularly earns you credits
4. Submitting Jobs before the deadline also earn you credits

5. Bad Editor reviews reduce credits
6. Bad Client reviews reduce credits.
7. Missing submission deadlines reduce your credits. Five such offences will result in account deactivation.



Credits

WhitePanda uses a credit based system to judge a writer’s performance .On the accumulation of a sufficient number of credits, the writer becomes eligible for a promotion. Credits are added or deducted after each job based on the writer’s performance on that particular job. Credits may also be earned by taking jobs more frequently.



Jobs

A writer may work on only one assignment at a time. He/she has 48 hours to submit the job to White Panda. If the client deems the job satisfactory, your fee shall be added to your account 72 hours after the job is submitted to the client.



Writer Account

A Writer may view his/her account in the account section. The writer may choose to withdraw this amount at any time between 5pm and 11pm. We at the moment shall only be able to process requests over Rs 500.



Types of assignments

Whitepanda at the moment offers the following four types of assignments

1. Blog Posts (350 – 450 words)
2. Articles (750 – 850 words)
3. Facebook Posts (10 posts ,2 sentences each) - 200/-
4. Tweets (10 tweets, 140 characters each) 200/-
