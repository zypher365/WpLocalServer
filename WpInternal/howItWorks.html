Quality Control

Since quality is our top priority, we have designed a complex series of tests to ensure only the best writers gain access to your projects. And since that is not always enough, we have also rounded up a team of copy-editors to gleefully point out work hard to fix the rare instances of bad writing, factual errors and plagiarism that may occasionally sneak in. However, all this happens behind the scenes. So, all you see is perfect copy.

Technology

Delivering high quality-content to clients is our number one priority. Our team of copy editors checks for spelling, plagiarism, and more.

72 hours revision period

As much as we hate to admit it, sometimes, glitches do occur for reasons beyond our control. Which is why we offer all our clients a 72 hour revision period. If you are not satisfied with any aspect of the content we have sent you, please send it back with instructions, and we will send you a reworked copy.
